# About

This playbook was developed by Abhishek Parmar as part of applied 
learning during a PGD in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics at 
Rashtriya Raksha University in India.

It is based on the open-source IR plan template by Counteractive 
Security, adapted specifically for the threat environment faced by 
educational and government-affiliated institutions — including 
CERT-In coordination, MHA liaison protocols, and non-technical 
stakeholder involvement in incident response.

The tabletop exercise scenario (Operation Locked Campus) was 
independently developed to address gaps observed in how academic 
institutions plan for ransomware and data exfiltration events.
